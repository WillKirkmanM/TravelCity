Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking?

Visit your booking dashboard and click the cancel button. Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and various local payment methods depending on your country.

How do I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team 24/7 through chat, email, or phone.
