Our Commitment to Fighting Discrimination

At TravelCity, we believe that travel should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to creating an inclusive platform where all travelers feel welcome, regardless of their race, color, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, age, or disability.

Zero Tolerance Policy

We immediately investigate all reports of discrimination and take appropriate action, including permanent removal from our platform.

24/7 Support

Our dedicated support team is available around the clock to assist with any discrimination-related concerns.

How We Combat Discrimination

Proactive Prevention

We use advanced systems to detect and prevent discriminatory behavior before it occurs.

Swift Action

Immediate investigation of all reported incidents with transparent communication.

Community Education

Regular training and resources for our community members on inclusive practices.

Report Discrimination

If you experience or witness discrimination:

  1. Use the "Report" button on any listing or profile
  2. Contact our 24/7 support team
  3. Document the incident with screenshots or messages
