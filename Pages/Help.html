Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, go to the Flights section and enter your travel details. Choose a flight, then complete your payment.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, cancellations can be made directly from your account settings. Note that some bookings are non-refundable.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact support through the Contact Us page, or call our support line available 24/7.
